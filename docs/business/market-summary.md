# Market summary

Updated: 2026-07-05

Wexio is positioned as a modular business workspace for SMEs.

## Audience

The first audience is companies that use several disconnected tools and want clearer work around users, documents, roles, tasks, and business records.

## First segments

Good first segments include local SMEs, service companies, field teams, consultants, retail operations, and companies that need better document and workflow control.

## First use case

The first use case should be a limited company workspace with users, documents, tasks, clients, products, time tracking, HR basics, and invoicing workflow preparation.

## Growth rule

Wexio should expand after pilot users confirm clear value.
