# Pilot outreach message

Updated: 2026-07-04

This page gives public wording for explaining Wexio to a potential pilot company.

## Short positioning

Wexio is a business workspace for companies that want to reduce scattered tools and bring users, documents, modules, and workflows into one structured environment.

## Simple message

Most companies do not need one more isolated application. They need a clearer way to work.

Wexio helps test whether a company can bring daily business work into one workspace: clients, documents, tasks, users, time tracking, HR, finance, invoicing, and reporting.

The controlled pilot is used to validate real usefulness with selected users before wider rollout.

## Conversation opener

Hello,

I am building Wexio, a modular business workspace for companies.

The goal is not to add another disconnected tool. The goal is to help a company centralize daily work in one place: users, roles, documents, modules, workflows, and operational follow-up.

I am looking for selected pilot companies to test Wexio on a clear, limited scope and measure whether it brings practical value.

## What to propose

A good first discussion should focus on:

- which tools the company uses today;
- where information is scattered;
- which team members should test first;
- which modules would bring value first;
- what success would look like after a short pilot.

## Safe wording

Use:

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations.

Avoid promising every module or every external provider workflow before it has been validated for the specific pilot.
