# Controlled pilot status

Updated: 2026-07-04

## Public decision

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations.

This status means selected company workspaces and selected users may be used for controlled demonstrations and pilot conversations.

It does not mean unrestricted public production rollout.

## What is ready to show

The public pilot demonstration can focus on:

- the company workspace model;
- the desktop-style user experience;
- users, roles, and different work surfaces;
- module navigation;
- documents and controlled sharing;
- widgets and shortcuts;
- active pilot modules such as time tracking, HR, finance, invoicing, and internal Peppol surfaces;
- public explanation of workspace separation and access boundaries.

## What must stay clear

The pilot should not claim that every Wexio module is ready for every company.

The pilot should not claim that provider-dependent workflows are already proven unless a separate provider setup and proof has been completed.

## Why the status matters

This boundary keeps the product credible. It allows Wexio to show real progress while still being honest about remaining validation, legal review, provider configuration, and wider rollout gates.

## Best public wording

Use: "Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations."
