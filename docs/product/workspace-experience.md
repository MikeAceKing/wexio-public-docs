# Workspace experience

Updated: 2026-07-04

Wexio is built around a browser workspace.

The idea is simple: a company should not need one separate product for every daily business activity. The workspace gives users one place to open modules, view widgets, work with documents, and move between operational tasks.

## What the user sees

A workspace user can work with:

- a personal desktop;
- module windows;
- widgets;
- shortcuts;
- document views;
- dashboards;
- role-specific screens.

## Why this matters

Many SMEs keep customer data, files, invoices, tasks, staff information, and reports in different tools. Wexio tries to connect those areas around one company context.

This does not mean every module is active for every company. A controlled pilot selects the useful modules first and validates them with real users.

## Practical example

A company may start with documents, clients, users, workflow, time tracking, and invoicing. Later it can evaluate HR, finance, products, reporting, or other operational modules.

## Pilot goal

The pilot should prove whether users can work faster, find information more easily, and trust the structure of the workspace.
