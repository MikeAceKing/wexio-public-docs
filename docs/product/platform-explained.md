# Platform explained

Updated: 2026-07-04

SBCOS by Wexio is a browser-first business workspace.

## Main idea

A company often has work spread across many tools: clients in one place, documents in another, invoices elsewhere, and tasks in a separate system.

Wexio aims to reduce that split by giving the company one structured workspace.

## Product layers

### Public layer

Explains Wexio and supports controlled demonstration requests.

### Company workspace

Groups the company's users, modules, records, documents, and workflows.

### Personal desktop

Gives each internal user a personal working area with widgets, shortcuts, and active modules.

### Business modules

Modules cover areas such as users, documents, clients, products, workflow, projects, support, time tracking, HR, finance, invoicing, reporting, inventory, and logistics.

### Control layer

Keeps roles, workspace boundaries, and module access organised.

## Current rollout

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations. The pilot approach validates value step by step before wider rollout.
