# Frequently asked questions

Updated: 2026-07-04

## What is Wexio?

Wexio is the company and master brand behind SBCOS by Wexio.

## What is SBCOS by Wexio?

SBCOS is a modular business cloud workspace for users, roles, modules, documents, workflows, and operational data.

## What is the current status?

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations only.

## Are all modules available to every company?

No. Module availability depends on pilot scope, activation, role, permission, configuration, and release status.

## Does this repository contain the application?

No. This repository is public documentation only.

## Who owns Wexio?

Wexio is created and owned by Michael Martin Belber.
