# Frequently asked questions

## What is Wexio?

Wexio is the company and master brand behind a modular business cloud workspace.

## What is SBCOS?

SBCOS by Wexio means SaaS Business Cloud Operating System: the workspace product that combines identity, modules, documents, data, and workflows.

## Who is Wexio for?

The initial focus is selected Belgian and Benelux SMEs participating in controlled demonstrations and pilots.

## Is Wexio an ERP?

Wexio includes ERP-style business areas, but its direction is broader: a role-aware operating workspace with personal desktops and connected operational modules.

## How is Wexio different from Odoo?

Wexio emphasizes a personal cloud-desktop experience, tenant/user boundaries, and selected modular workflows. It does not claim to reproduce or replace every Odoo capability today.

## Is Wexio ready for production?

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations. This does not authorize unrestricted public production rollout.

## What does “controlled pilot” mean?

A selected company, user group, module set, data scope, support process, and validation plan are agreed in advance. A pilot is not an unrestricted self-service launch.

## Which modules are available first?

The first set depends on the pilot. Workspace, users/access, documents, clients, products, projects, workflows, and selected operational modules may be considered after validation.

## Is Peppol available?

Peppol exists as an internal Wexio module. External network transmission requires separate provider configuration and recorded proof and is not yet provider-proven.

## Is Mollie available?

Mollie is not configured.

## Where is data stored?

Wexio uses managed cloud infrastructure. Contract-specific hosting location, subprocessors, backup, and retention details must be confirmed in the applicable customer agreement and data-processing terms.

## How is tenant data protected?

The security model includes server-resolved tenant context, memberships, role and permission checks, private documents, and database row-level controls for sensitive data.

## Can Wexio replace existing tools?

A pilot should first identify which tools and workflows can be consolidated safely. Wexio does not claim that every existing system can be replaced immediately.

## How much does Wexio cost?

Pilot and commercial pricing depends on users, modules, onboarding, support, and provider requirements. Request a written proposal.

## How do I request a demonstration?

Contact `admin@wexio.be` with your company, team size, current tools, and the workflow you want to improve.

## What happens during onboarding?

Wexio confirms scope, creates the tenant, configures users and roles, enables selected modules, reviews data handling, and defines pilot success criteria.

## Which languages are supported?

Wexio is designed for French, Dutch, and English contexts. Translation completeness may vary by surface during the controlled pilot phase.
