# What is Wexio?

Wexio is the company and master brand. **SBCOS by Wexio** is its modular SaaS Business Cloud Operating System.

SBCOS is designed as a secure cloud workspace in which a company can organize:

- tenant and company context;
- users, memberships, roles, and permissions;
- personal desktop layouts and widgets;
- enabled business modules;
- private and shared documents;
- operational records and workflows.

## More than a collection of apps

Wexio is not presented as a simple ERP clone. Its product direction is a connected workspace where permissions, modules, documents, and workflows share one tenant context while every internal user keeps a personal workspace.

## Intended audience

The initial focus is selected Belgian and Benelux SMEs that want a controlled way to consolidate business work. Each pilot must define its users, enabled modules, data scope, onboarding needs, and success criteria.

## Current boundary

Wexio is ready for selected controlled pilot demonstrations. This does not authorize unrestricted public production rollout.

Module availability varies by tenant, role, configuration, and validation status. External provider operation is never implied by the presence of an internal module.
